Before your team processes a single order, take a few minutes to complete your shop setup. This section walks you through every tab inside **System Setup** — from your business profile and bank details to printer configuration and receipt appearance. Getting these settings right upfront ensures that receipts print correctly, notifications reach the right people, and your brand is represented consistently across the POS interface.

<Warning>
  Each tab in System Setup has its own **Save** button. Changes you make on one tab are **not** automatically saved when you switch to another. Always click the save button on each tab before moving on, or your changes will be lost.
</Warning>

## Shop Profile

Your shop profile contains the core business information that appears on receipts, in reports, and on the customer-facing display.

Navigate to **Settings > System Setup** and select the **Profile** tab. Fill in the following fields:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Shop Name** | Your trading name as it appears on receipts and the dashboard. |
| **Phone Number** | Primary contact number printed on receipts. |
| **Email Address** | Business email used for notifications and customer communications. |
| **Address** | Physical address printed on receipts and invoices. |
| **Logo** | Upload a PNG or JPG (recommended: 300 × 300 px, max 2 MB). |
| **Color Scheme** | Choose a primary colour for the POS interface. |

Click **Save Profile** when all fields are complete.

<Tip>
  Use the **Color Scheme** picker to set your brand's primary colour — the same hex value used across your marketing materials. The colour you choose is applied to buttons, highlights, and active states throughout the entire POS interface, giving staff a branded, cohesive experience from the moment they log in. BasePoint POS uses **#0c6df6** as the default colour.
</Tip>

## Bank Details

Bank details appear on printed invoices and formal receipts, making it easier for corporate customers to process payments by bank transfer.

Navigate to **Settings > System Setup** and select the **Bank Details** tab. Enter the following:

- **Bank Name** — the full name of your business bank (for example, *Equity Bank Kenya*).
- **Account Number** — your business current account number.
- **Branch** — the branch name or code where the account is held.

Click **Save Bank Details**. These fields are optional for cash-only businesses but recommended for any outlet that accepts invoice payments or bank transfers.

## Printer Settings

BasePoint POS supports thermal receipt printers for fast, low-cost receipt printing at the counter.

Navigate to **Settings > System Setup** and select the **Printer Settings** tab. Configure the following:

- **Printer Name** — the name of the printer as it appears on your operating system.
- **Paper Width** — select **80mm** for standard thermal receipt rolls (the most common size for hospitality printers).
- **Print Mode** — choose between *Automatic* (prints immediately on order completion) and *Manual* (staff trigger printing from the order screen).
- **Number of Copies** — set how many receipt copies print per transaction (typically 1 for customers, 2 if you retain a merchant copy).

Click **Save Printer Settings**.

<Note>
  If you're running BasePoint POS on a Linux machine, your thermal printer may require a **CUPS (Common Unix Printing System)** driver to be installed and configured before it appears in the printer list. Consult your printer manufacturer's documentation for the correct CUPS PPD file, then ensure the printer is shared on your local network so the POS can reach it.
</Note>

## Receipt Appearance

Customise what your printed and digital receipts look like to reinforce your brand and include the information your customers need.

Navigate to **Settings > System Setup** and select the **Receipt Appearance** tab. You can configure:

- **Receipt Header** — a short line of text printed at the top of every receipt (for example, your slogan or a thank-you message such as *"Thank you for dining with us!"*).
- **Receipt Footer** — a closing message or legal disclaimer printed at the bottom (for example, WiFi password, returns policy, or social media handles).
- **Show/Hide Fields** — toggle individual receipt fields on or off, including tax breakdown, cashier name, table number, order number, and customer name.
- **Logo on Receipt** — toggle whether your shop logo prints at the top of receipts (requires a logo to be uploaded in the Profile tab).

Click **Save Receipt Settings** when your layout looks the way you want. Use the built-in **Preview** button to see a sample receipt before committing to the layout.

## Notification Settings

BasePoint POS can alert you and your team when key events occur — such as low stock, completed sales, or shift summaries.

Navigate to **Settings > System Setup** and select the **Notification Settings** tab. Configure your preferred alert channels:

- **Email Notifications** — enter one or more email addresses to receive daily sales summaries, low-stock alerts, and end-of-shift reports.
- **WhatsApp Alerts** — enter a WhatsApp-enabled phone number to receive real-time transaction confirmations and critical alerts directly in WhatsApp.

Toggle each notification type on or off individually so your team only receives the alerts that are relevant to their role.

Click **Save Notification Settings** to apply your choices.
