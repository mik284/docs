Staff analytics give you an objective, data-backed picture of how your team is performing — who is driving the most revenue, how consistently staff are showing up, and how leave balances are tracking. Use this data to recognize top performers, support struggling team members, and make informed decisions about scheduling and incentives.

<Note>
  Staff analytics are scoped to the shop you're currently logged into. Users with admin access and multi-shop permissions can view consolidated cross-shop staff data from the Admin Dashboard.
</Note>

## Staff Sales Performance

Navigate to **Reports**, then select the **Top Earners** report tab to view sales attributed to each staff member.

BasePoint POS attributes a transaction to the staff member who was logged in when the sale was processed. The Top Earners report shows each staff member's total sales revenue for the selected date range, ranked from highest to lowest. You can also configure the staff and platform revenue share split to calculate each team member's earnings contribution.

To generate the report:

1. Go to **Reports** in the main sidebar.
2. Select the **Top Earners** tab.
3. Set your date and time range.
4. Optionally select a specific branch.
5. Set the staff and platform revenue share percentages (they must total 100%).
6. Click **Generate Top Earners**.

<Tip>
  Use the Top Earners report during staff reviews and one-on-ones to celebrate strong performance with specific data. Recognizing high earners publicly — even a simple "top seller of the month" announcement — has a measurable positive effect on team morale and healthy competition.
</Tip>

## Shift Schedule and Attendance

Navigate to **Employee Shift** from the main menu to manage and review your team's schedule and attendance records. This section has several tabs:

### Shifts

The **Shifts** tab shows the shift calendar for your team. You can see which staff members are scheduled and manage upcoming rotas from here.

### Attendance

The **Attendance** tab (available when the Bandu HR module is enabled) shows each staff member's attendance history for the selected period, including:

- **Present** — days the staff member clocked in on time
- **Late** — days where clock-in was recorded after the scheduled shift start
- **Absent** — scheduled shift days with no clock-in record
- **On Leave** — days covered by an approved leave request
- **Actual hours worked** — derived from clock-in and clock-out timestamps
- **Overtime hours** — hours worked beyond the scheduled shift end
- **Attendance rate** — percentage of working days where the staff member was present or late

Use the date range picker to scope the view to the period you want to review.

<Accordion title="What if a staff member forgot to clock in or out?">
  Managers with the appropriate permissions can manually adjust attendance records. Locate the record in the Attendance tab, edit the clock-in or clock-out time, and save with a note explaining the correction. All manual edits are logged for audit purposes.
</Accordion>

## Leave Balances

The **My Leave** tab (available when the Bandu HR module is enabled) shows each staff member's leave entitlements and remaining balances for the current period. Leave types include Annual, Sick, Emergency, Maternity, Paternity, and Unpaid leave, depending on your HR configuration.

Managers can review leave requests and balances from the **Admin** section. Staff members can view their own leave balance and submit new requests directly from the Employee Shift page.

<Info>
  The Attendance, Leave, and Payroll features require the **Bandu HR** module. Enable it from the **Discover** page to unlock the full suite of HR tools, including shift scheduling, leave management, and attendance tracking.
</Info>
