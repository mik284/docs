Staff analytics give you an objective, data-backed picture of how your team is performing — who is driving the most revenue, how actual hours worked compare to the schedule, and whether attendance is consistent. Use this data to recognize top performers, support struggling team members, and make informed decisions about scheduling and incentives.

<Note>
  Staff analytics are scoped to the shop you're currently logged into. Users with admin access and multi-shop permissions can view consolidated cross-shop staff data from the Admin Dashboard.
</Note>

## Staff Sales Performance

Navigate to **Reports > Top Sellers** to view sales attributed to each staff member.

BasePoint POS attributes a transaction to the staff member who was logged in (via their credentials or PIN) when the sale was processed. The Top Sellers report shows:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Staff Member** | Name and role of the team member |
| **Number of Transactions** | Total count of sales processed by this staff member in the period |
| **Total Revenue** | Sum of all transaction values attributed to them |
| **Average Order Value** | Total revenue ÷ number of transactions |
| **Rank** | Position in the leaderboard sorted by total revenue or transaction count |

Switch between **Revenue** and **Quantity** sorting using the **Sort By** dropdown at the top of the report. Apply a date range filter to review performance over any period.

<Tip>
  Use the Top Sellers report during staff reviews and one-on-ones to celebrate strong performance with specific data. Recognizing high earners publicly — even a simple "top seller of the month" announcement — has a measurable positive effect on team morale and healthy competition.
</Tip>

## Shift Hours Report

Compare scheduled hours against the hours staff actually worked.

1. Navigate to **Employee Shift** from the main menu.
2. Open the **Analytics** tab.
3. Apply a date range to scope the report to the period you want to review.

The shift hours report shows:

- **Scheduled hours** — the hours you assigned to each staff member on the shift calendar
- **Actual hours** — derived from clock-in and clock-out timestamps
- **Variance** — the difference between scheduled and actual, flagged when significant
- **Overtime hours** — hours worked beyond the scheduled shift

Export the report to CSV for import into your payroll software or to share with your accountant.

## Clock-In Attendance

Monitor day-by-day attendance records from **Admin > Staff Clock-In**.

The attendance view shows:

- All staff who clocked in on the selected date
- Exact clock-in and clock-out times
- Late arrivals (highlighted when clock-in is after the scheduled shift start)
- Early departures (highlighted when clock-out is before the scheduled shift end)
- Absences — staff members with a scheduled shift who have no clock-in record

**To review attendance for a specific period:**

1. Navigate to **Admin > Staff Clock-In**.
2. Use the date range picker to select the period.
3. Optionally filter by staff member.
4. Review the attendance table or export to CSV.

<Accordion title="What if a staff member forgot to clock in or out?">
  Managers can manually edit clock-in and clock-out times from the Staff Clock-In admin view. Click the pencil icon on any record row, adjust the time, and save with a note explaining the manual correction. All edits are logged for audit purposes.
</Accordion>

## Wages Reporting

Navigate to **Admin > Wages** to review wage records and payment status for each staff member.

The Wages report includes:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Staff Member** | Name and department |
| **Pay Period** | The period covered by this wage record |
| **Hours Worked** | Total hours from the shift analytics for the period |
| **Gross Wage** | Calculated amount based on hourly rate or fixed salary |
| **Deductions** | Any recorded deductions (advances, penalties) |
| **Net Payable** | Final amount to pay |
| **Payment Status** | Pending / Processing / Paid |

Filter by period and staff member to view individual payment histories. Export to CSV for your accountant or payroll processor.

<Info>
  If your subscription includes the **Bandu Payroll** module, the full payroll run — including tax calculations, statutory deductions, and pay slips — is handled in the Bandu section with direct integration to shift hours and leave balances.
</Info>
