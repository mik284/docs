Sales reports give you deep visibility into your revenue performance — from the grand total down to individual product lines, fulfilment types, and tax summaries. Use them to understand what's selling, when business peaks, and where money is coming in from, so you can make informed decisions about your menu, staffing, and promotions.

## Accessing Reports

Navigate to **Reports** in the main left sidebar. The Reports page organizes report types into tabs based on the modules enabled on your account:

- **Duka (Store)** — POS sales reports, available by default
- **Pesa (Accounting)** — accounting reports, shown when the Pesa accounting module is enabled
- **Mteja (CRM)** — customer relationship reports, shown when the Mteja CRM module is enabled
- **Bandu (HR)** — HR and payroll reports, shown when the Bandu HR module is enabled
- **Dala (Real Estate)** — property and lease reports, shown when the Dala real estate module is enabled

The sections below cover the **Duka (Store)** POS sales reports.

## Report Types

<Accordion title="Item Sales Report">
  A detailed breakdown of every item sold within the selected date and time range.

  - **Filters:** Date and time range, branch, staff member who created the sale, service location, and optional commission rate
  - **Use for:** Reconciling what was sold against what was rung up, identifying discrepancies, and calculating staff commission on specific items

  Select your date range, optionally narrow by branch or staff member, then click **Generate Item Sales Report** to open the report view.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sales Report">
  A consolidated summary of all completed transactions — total revenue, order count, and a line-by-line breakdown of every sale — for the selected period and branch.

  - **Filters:** Date and time range, branch
  - **Use for:** End-of-day or end-of-period reconciliation, comparing revenue across branches, and providing a printable summary for your accountant

  Click **Generate Sales Report** to open the formatted report.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Voided Bills Report">
  A record of every transaction that was voided within the selected period.

  - **Filters:** Date and time range, branch
  - **Columns:** Transaction reference, voiding staff member, original amount, reason (where recorded)
  - **Use for:** Auditing unusual void activity, spotting potential misuse, and reconciling cash drawer discrepancies

  Click **Generate Voided Report** to review voided transactions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Delivery Report">
  Break down orders by fulfilment type across the selected date range.

  - **Filters:** Date and time range, branch
  - **Use for:** Comparing delivery order volume against dine-in and takeaway, evaluating delivery channel performance

  Click **Generate Delivery Report** to open the delivery breakdown.

  <Info>
    The Delivery Report is only available if the delivery module or a delivery platform integration is enabled on your account.
  </Info>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Inventory Usage Report">
  Shows which inventory items were consumed across all orders in the selected period.

  - **Filters:** Date and time range, branch
  - **Use for:** Tracking ingredient consumption against purchases, spotting wastage, and informing stock order quantities

  Click **Generate Inventory Usage Report** to view consumption figures.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="VAT Summary Report">
  A summary of VAT collected across all taxable transactions in the selected period.

  - **Filters:** Date and time range, branch
  - **Columns:** Gross sales, taxable amount, VAT collected, net amount
  - **Use for:** Preparing VAT returns, sharing tax figures with your accountant, and confirming tax-inclusive pricing is applied correctly

  Click **Generate VAT Report** to view the tax summary.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Top Earners Report">
  A ranked list of staff members by total sales value generated during the selected period.

  - **Filters:** Date and time range, branch, staff revenue share percentage, platform revenue share percentage
  - **Use for:** Identifying your highest-performing team members, calculating commission splits between staff and the platform, and running staff performance reviews

  Set the staff and platform revenue share percentages (they must total 100%), then click **Generate Top Earners** to view the ranked results.
</Accordion>

## Filtering Reports

Each report uses a consistent filter panel. Available filters vary slightly by report type:

| Filter | Options |
|---|---|
| **Date and Time Range** | Today, Yesterday, This Week, Last Week, This Month, Custom Range — with optional start and end times |
| **Branch** | All branches (shown at admin level) or a specific location |
| **Staff Member** | Limit the Item Sales report to a specific cashier or server |
| **Service Location** | Narrow Item Sales to a specific table area or service zone |
| **Commission Rate** | Apply a commission percentage to Item Sales calculations |

Set your filters and click the **Generate** button for the relevant report to load the data.

## Exporting Reports

Each generated report can be saved for offline analysis or sharing:

1. Apply your desired filters and generate the report.
2. Use the **Print** or **Export** option within the report view.
3. Choose your preferred format:
   - **CSV** — opens in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet application for further analysis
   - **PDF** — formatted printable document for sharing with stakeholders or filing

<Tip>
  If you manage multiple branches, navigate to the **Admin Dashboard** and open **Reports** from there to filter across all locations in a single report, rather than generating each branch separately.
</Tip>
