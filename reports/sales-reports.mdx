Sales reports give you deep visibility into your revenue performance — from the grand total down to individual product lines, payment methods, and service types. Use them to understand what's selling, when business peaks, and where money is coming in from, so you can make informed decisions about your menu, staffing, and promotions.

## Accessing Reports

Navigate to **Reports** in the main left sidebar. The unified Reports page organizes report types into tabs. Depending on your enabled modules, you may see tabs for:

- **Duka** (POS sales reports — available by default)
- **Pesa** (accounting reports — if the accounting module is enabled)
- **Mteja** (CRM reports — if Mteja CRM is enabled)
- **Bandu** (HR and payroll reports — if the payroll module is enabled)

The sections below cover the **Duka** (POS) sales reports.

## Report Types

<Accordion title="Item Sales Report">
  View the sales quantity and total revenue generated by each individual product or menu item.

  - **Columns:** Item name, category, quantity sold, unit price, total revenue
  - **Use for:** Identifying slow-moving inventory, planning menu changes, negotiating with suppliers based on volume

  Filter by date range, product category, or specific item to drill down into the data.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Sales by Period">
  See your revenue broken down by time — daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly.

  - **Chart view:** Line or bar chart showing revenue trend over the selected period
  - **Table view:** Day-by-day or period-by-period breakdown of transaction count and total revenue
  - **Use for:** Identifying peak trading days, comparing month-on-month growth, spotting seasonal patterns

  Toggle between chart and table views using the view switcher at the top of the report.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Top Sellers Report">
  A ranked list of your best-performing products, sorted by revenue or quantity sold.

  - **Sort options:** Sort by total revenue or by units sold
  - **Hierarchy view:** See top categories first, then drill into each category for top items
  - **Analytics view:** Visual bar chart of performance across your product range
  - **Use for:** Featuring popular items on your menu or shelf, making purchasing decisions, staff training on best-sellers

  Use the **Category** filter to view top sellers within a specific product group.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Payment Method Breakdown">
  Understand how your customers pay and what proportion of revenue comes from each method.

  - **Columns:** Payment method, number of transactions, total amount, percentage of revenue
  - **Use for:** Reconciling cash counts, verifying mobile money settlements, identifying whether to invest in additional card terminals

  Compare payment method splits across different time periods to spot shifts in customer payment behaviour.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Delivery Report">
  Break down orders by fulfilment type — dine-in, takeaway, and delivery.

  - **Columns:** Fulfilment type, order count, revenue, average order value
  - **Delivery platform breakdown:** If third-party delivery platforms are integrated, revenue per platform is shown separately
  - **Use for:** Comparing the profitability of delivery vs dine-in, evaluating delivery partner performance

  <Info>
    The Delivery Report is only available if the delivery module or a delivery platform integration is enabled on your account.
  </Info>
</Accordion>

## Filtering Reports

Every report supports the following filters:

| Filter | Options |
|---|---|
| **Date Range** | Today, Yesterday, This Week, Last Week, This Month, Last Month, Custom Range |
| **Shop** | All shops (admin level) or a specific location |
| **Staff Member** | Filter transactions to a specific cashier or waiter |
| **Payment Method** | Limit results to a specific payment type |
| **Category** | Narrow to a specific product category |

Apply filters using the panel at the top of each report, then click **Apply** to refresh the data.

## Exporting Reports

Download any report for offline analysis or sharing:

1. Apply your desired filters to scope the report to the data you need.
2. Click the **Export** button in the top-right corner of the report.
3. Choose your format:
   - **CSV** — opens in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet application for further analysis
   - **PDF** — formatted printable document for sharing with stakeholders or filing

<Tip>
  If you manage multiple shop locations, navigate to the **Admin Dashboard > Reports** section (available to users with admin access) to view consolidated cross-shop analytics in a single report, rather than exporting each location separately.
</Tip>

## Scheduled Email Reports

Receive reports automatically in your inbox without logging in:

1. Navigate to **Settings > System Setup > Notification Settings**.
2. Enable **Daily Sales Summary** and/or **Weekly Summary**.
3. Add the recipient email addresses.
4. Save.

The daily summary is sent each evening and includes that day's total revenue, transaction count, and top-selling items.
