Pesa is the built-in accounting module in BasePoint POS. It connects directly to your point-of-sale activity so that every completed sale automatically becomes an income entry — no duplicate data entry, no separate accounting software required. Use Pesa to manage your chart of accounts, post journal entries, reconcile bank statements, record expenses, and monitor your business's financial health from a single dashboard.

## What Pesa Tracks

Pesa gives you a complete picture of your business finances by consolidating all financial activity in one place:

- **Sales revenue** — automatically imported from every completed POS transaction
- **Expenses** — manually entered costs such as rent, utilities, salaries, and supplies
- **Bills from suppliers** — invoices received and tracked through to payment
- **Bank statement imports** — uploaded statements matched against your recorded entries
- **Journal entries** — manual adjustments for depreciation, accruals, corrections, and equity movements

<Note>
  POS sales are automatically recorded as income entries in Pesa the moment an order is completed. You do not need to enter sales manually — they appear in Accounting > Sales Receipts in real time.
</Note>

## Accounting Dashboard

The Pesa dashboard gives you an at-a-glance view of your financial position. Access it by clicking the **Pesa** tab on the BasePoint POS home dashboard.

The dashboard displays:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Revenue** | Cumulative income for the selected period |
| **Expenses** | All recorded expenses and bills for the period |
| **Net Profit / Loss** | Revenue minus expenses — positive is profit, negative is a loss |
| **Total Assets** | The combined value of all asset accounts at the end of the period |

Use the period selector (Day, Week, Month, Year, or Custom) at the top of the dashboard to adjust the reporting window. Charts show revenue and expense trends over the last six months so you can spot patterns quickly. The dashboard also shows a summary of your recent journal entries, reconciliation status, receivables, payables, cash positions, and VAT position.

## Multi-Currency Support

Pesa supports transactions in multiple currencies, making it suitable for businesses that deal with international suppliers or customers.

To add a currency and set its exchange rate:

1. Navigate to **Accounting > Currencies**.
2. Click **Add Currency**.
3. Select the currency code and enter the current exchange rate against your base currency.
4. Save.

Your **base currency** is configured in **System Setup > General Settings**. All reports and dashboards display values in the base currency, with original transaction amounts shown alongside converted figures.

<Tip>
  Reconcile your bank statement at the end of every month. Monthly reconciliation catches discrepancies — such as bank fees, duplicate entries, or missed transactions — before they compound and become harder to trace.
</Tip>
