## What's Included

BasePoint POS is a multi-module platform. Each module is purpose-built to handle a distinct area of your business, and all modules share the same data so nothing falls through the cracks.

- **Duka POS** — The core point-of-sale engine. Ring up orders, manage tables and floors, apply discounts, split bills, and close shifts with full audit trails.
- **Pesa Accounting** — A full double-entry accounting suite. Track income and expenses, reconcile bank statements, generate profit-and-loss reports, and manage your chart of accounts.
- **Mteja CRM** — Build lasting customer relationships. Capture guest profiles, track purchase history, run loyalty programs, and send targeted promotions.
- **Bandu HR** — Manage your workforce end-to-end. Handle employee records, attendance, leave requests, payroll runs, and role-based access from one place.
- **Dala Real Estate** — Purpose-built for property-linked businesses. Manage units, leases, and rental income alongside your day-to-day hospitality operations.

## How Onboarding Works

Follow these four steps to get your business live on BasePoint POS. Each step builds on the last, so work through them in order.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up at [hospitality.reliatech.co.ke](https://hospitality.reliatech.co.ke) using your business email. Your account becomes the owner-level Admin for your organisation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Shop">
    Navigate to **System Setup** and fill in your shop profile — name, contact details, logo, and branding. Add your bank details and configure your receipt layout so you're ready to print from day one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your Menu or Products">
    Head to **Settings > Category Settings** to create product categories, then add your menu items or retail products under **Store > Add Product**. Set prices, link categories, and upload product images.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Taking Orders">
    Open the **Tables** view, select a table, browse your menu, add items to the cart, and process your first payment. Your POS is live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
