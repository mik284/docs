The Omnichannel Inbox consolidates customer messages from WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and Instagram into a single, unified view inside BasePoint POS. Instead of switching between three different apps to respond to customers, your team handles every conversation in one place — with channel filters, assignment tools, and status tracking built in.

<Note>
  Access to the Omnichannel Inbox requires the `OMNICHANNEL_VIEW` permission on the logged-in user's role. Ensure this permission is enabled for the relevant staff roles under **Settings > Staff & Users > Roles**.
</Note>

## Connecting a Channel

Connect each messaging channel once, and all incoming conversations flow into the shared inbox automatically.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="WhatsApp">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the channel connection screen">
        Navigate to the **Omnichannel** page in the main navigation and click **Connect Channel**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select WhatsApp">
        Choose **WhatsApp** from the list of available channels.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Log in with Facebook / Meta">
        Click **Connect via Facebook / Meta**. A browser popup opens where you log in with the Facebook account that manages your WhatsApp Business account and grant the requested permissions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select your WhatsApp Business account">
        Choose the WhatsApp Business Account and the specific phone number you want to connect.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm the connection">
        Click **Finish**. WhatsApp is now connected and messages will appear in the Omnichannel Inbox.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Facebook Messenger">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the channel connection screen">
        Navigate to the **Omnichannel** page and click **Connect Channel**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Messenger">
        Choose **Facebook Messenger** from the channel list.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Log in with Facebook">
        Click **Connect via Facebook**. A browser popup opens where you log in with the Facebook account that manages your Business Page and approve the requested permissions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select your Page">
        Choose the Facebook Page whose Messenger inbox you want to connect.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm the connection">
        Click **Finish**. Messenger is now connected and messages will appear in the inbox.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Instagram">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the channel connection screen">
        Navigate to the **Omnichannel** page and click **Connect Channel**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Instagram">
        Choose **Instagram** from the channel list.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Log in with Facebook / Instagram">
        Click **Connect via Facebook / Instagram**. A browser popup opens where you log in and grant permissions for your Instagram Business account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select your Instagram account">
        Pick the Instagram Business account you want to link. It must be connected to a Facebook Page.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm the connection">
        Click **Finish**. Instagram is now connected and Direct Messages will appear in the inbox.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Info>
      Instagram Direct Message integration requires your Instagram account to be a **Business** or **Creator** account linked to a Facebook Page. Personal Instagram accounts cannot be connected.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Managing Conversations

Once channels are connected, all incoming messages appear in the **Omnichannel Inbox**. Use the filters at the top of the inbox to narrow the view:

- **Channel filter** — view All, WhatsApp only, Messenger only, or Instagram only
- **Status filter** — view All, Open, Pending, Resolved, or Closed conversations

Each conversation row shows the customer name (or number), the channel icon, the last message preview, and the timestamp of the last activity.

## Conversation Statuses

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Open" icon="message-circle" href="/settings/omnichannel">
    The conversation is active and requires a response. New inbound messages automatically set a conversation to Open.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pending" icon="clock" href="/settings/omnichannel">
    A reply has been sent and the conversation is waiting for the customer to respond.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Resolved" icon="circle-check" href="/settings/omnichannel">
    The customer's issue has been addressed and the conversation is marked as complete. Resolved conversations can be reopened if the customer replies.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Closed" icon="archive" href="/settings/omnichannel">
    The conversation is archived. Closed conversations are no longer visible in the active inbox but remain in history.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Replying to Messages

1. Click any conversation row to open it.
2. Read the full message thread in the conversation panel on the right.
3. Type your reply in the message box at the bottom of the panel.
4. Press **Enter** or click **Send**.

Replies support plain text and emoji.

## Assigning Conversations

Distribute workload across your team by assigning conversations to specific staff members:

1. Open a conversation.
2. Click the **Assign** button (person icon) in the conversation header.
3. Select the staff member from the dropdown.
4. The conversation moves to that staff member's assigned queue.

Staff members see conversations assigned to them and unassigned conversations, depending on their role permissions. Managers with the full Omnichannel permission see all conversations across all channels.

<Tip>
  Use the channel and status filters together — for example, filtering to WhatsApp + Open — to give each team member a focused queue of conversations to work through during their shift.
</Tip>
