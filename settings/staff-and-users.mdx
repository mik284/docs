Staff & Users is where you create and manage every person who works with BasePoint POS — from cashiers and waiters to managers and administrators. Give each team member their own account with the right level of access, configure permissions, and manage leave requests from one central location.

## Adding a Staff Member

Create an account for each team member before they start using the POS.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Staff & Users">
    Navigate to **Settings** in the left sidebar, then select **Staff & Users**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite or add a user">
    Click **Invite User** (to send an email invitation) or **Add User** (to create the account directly without an invitation).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the staff member's details">
    Fill in the user form:

    - **Full Name** — the staff member's name as it appears on receipts and reports
    - **Email Address** — used for login and system notifications
    - **Phone Number** — optional; used for WhatsApp notifications if configured
    - **Role** — the permission group that controls what this person can see and do (see [Managing Roles](#managing-roles) below)
    - **Department** — the team or department this person belongs to (e.g. Kitchen, Front of House, Management)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a POS PIN (optional)">
    Assign a 4- or 6-digit PIN for quick POS login. This allows the staff member to clock in and process sales on a shared terminal without typing a full email and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the account">
    Click **Save**. If you used **Invite User**, the staff member receives an email with a link to set their password. If you used **Add User**, you can share the credentials directly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use PINs on shared POS terminals so staff can quickly switch between users during busy periods. Each staff member taps their PIN, completes their transaction, and the next person can take over — no full logout/login required.
</Tip>

## Staff Login

Staff members can authenticate at the POS in two ways:

- **Email and password** — full login for admin access, settings changes, and report viewing
- **Quick-access PIN** — fast entry from the POS lock screen for cashiers and floor staff who only need to process sales

Encourage floor staff to always use their own PIN (not a shared login) so that sales are correctly attributed to the right person in reports and targets.

## Managing Roles

Roles define what a staff member can see and do inside BasePoint POS. Navigate to **Staff & Users**, then open the **Roles** tab to view, create, and edit roles.

Each role is a named permission set. You can create as many custom roles as your business needs. Common roles include:

| Role | Typical Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Cashier** | Process sales, view own shift, print receipts |
| **Waiter** | Take orders, view table status, no access to reports |
| **Supervisor** | Everything a cashier can do, plus view end-of-day reports and apply discounts |
| **Manager** | Full access except system configuration |
| **Admin** | Unrestricted access to all settings and data |

For a full breakdown of individual permissions, open the **Permissions** tab under **Staff & Users**.

## Departments

If your account has the CRM module enabled, a **Departments** tab appears under **Staff & Users**. Use it to create and manage the departments in your business (e.g. Kitchen, Front of House, Accounts) and assign staff members to the appropriate department for reporting and communication purposes.

## Leave Management

<Info>
  Leave management features are available when the **Bandu** payroll module is enabled on your account. Contact BasePoint POS support to activate this module.
</Info>

When Bandu is enabled, two additional tabs appear under **Staff & Users**:

**Leave Requests tab** — shows all staff leave requests with a live badge count of pending approvals. As a manager you can approve or reject each request with a single click. Approved leave is reflected automatically in attendance records.

**Leave Entitlements tab** — define how many days each leave type provides per year for your team. The following leave types are supported:

| Leave Type | Typical Use |
|---|---|
| **Annual** | Standard paid annual leave |
| **Sick** | Illness or medical appointments |
| **Emergency** | Urgent unforeseen circumstances |
| **Maternity** | Maternity leave for new mothers |
| **Paternity** | Paternity leave for new fathers |
| **Unpaid** | Leave taken without pay |

Balances are tracked per staff member and update automatically as leave requests are approved.

**For staff submitting requests:** Staff log in to the **Staff Leave Portal** to submit a leave request. They select the leave type, specify the start and end dates, and optionally add a reason. The portal also displays their remaining leave balance for each type. Pending requests can be withdrawn before a manager acts on them.

## Wages

Record and manage staff wage payments from **Admin > Wages**:

- Add a wage record for each pay period (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly)
- Mark payments as Pending, Processing, or Paid
- View a summary of total wage spend by staff member and period

Export wage records to CSV for your accountant or payroll software.

<Info>
  Full payroll processing — salary calculations, payslips, deductions, and tax summaries — is part of the **Bandu Payroll** module. When enabled, a **Payroll** tab appears under **Staff & Users**.
</Info>
