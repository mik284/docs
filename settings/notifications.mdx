Notifications let BasePoint POS automatically send messages to your customers — such as receipts and order confirmations — through the channels you choose. You can enable email, WhatsApp, or both, and control whether WhatsApp messages are sent in sandbox (testing) or production (live) mode.

## Notification Channels

Configure notification delivery in **Settings > System Setup > Notification Settings**.

Two delivery channels are available:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Email" icon="envelope" href="/settings/system-setup">
    Send automated messages to customers via email. Toggle this on to enable email delivery.
  </Card>
  <Card title="WhatsApp" icon="message-circle" href="/settings/system-setup">
    Send automated messages via WhatsApp. Requires a registered WhatsApp Business sender number. See [WhatsApp Registration](/settings/system-setup) to set up your sender.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**To configure notification channels:**

1. Navigate to **Settings > System Setup**.
2. Open the **Notification Settings** tab.
3. Toggle on the channels you want to use (**Email**, **WhatsApp**, or both).
4. Click **Save Changes**.

<Note>
  If you enable WhatsApp without a registered sender, you will be prompted to complete WhatsApp Registration before messages can be sent.
</Note>

## WhatsApp Mode

When WhatsApp is enabled, you must choose an operating mode:

| Mode | Description |
|---|---|
| **Sandbox** | For testing only. Messages are routed through a shared Twilio test number. Recipients must first opt in to the sandbox by sending a join message to the test number before they can receive messages. |
| **Production** | Live messaging using your verified WhatsApp Business number. Use this once your sender registration is complete and you are ready to communicate with real customers. |

To switch modes, open **Settings > System Setup > Notification Settings**, select the desired mode under **WhatsApp Mode Configuration**, and click **Save Changes**.

## WhatsApp Sender Registration

Before enabling WhatsApp notifications in production, register your business phone number as a WhatsApp sender:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open WhatsApp Registration">
    Navigate to **Settings > System Setup**, then open the **WhatsApp Registration** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your business details">
    Type your phone number in international format (e.g. +254712345678) and fill in your business name and optional details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and verify">
    Click **Register WhatsApp Sender**. Once the system processes your number, a 6-digit OTP is sent to the registered phone via WhatsApp. Enter it and click **Verify OTP**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set mode to Production">
    Return to the **Notification Settings** tab, set the WhatsApp mode to **Production**, and click **Save Changes**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once registered, customers who provide a WhatsApp-enabled phone number at checkout will automatically receive messages through the channels you have enabled.

## In-App Notifications

BasePoint POS also surfaces notifications directly inside the application:

- The **bell icon** in the top navigation bar shows a badge count of unread notifications.
- Click the bell to see a quick preview of recent alerts.
- Select **View All Notifications** to see the complete history, filter by type, and mark items as read.

In-app notifications include system alerts, approval requests (such as pending staff leave requests), and activity summaries relevant to the currently logged-in user's role.

<Tip>
  Use Production mode for all customer-facing messaging. Reserve Sandbox mode for internal testing before launching a new notification workflow to customers.
</Tip>
