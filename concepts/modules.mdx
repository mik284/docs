BasePoint POS follows a modular architecture, meaning your account only surfaces the tools that are relevant to your business. Instead of one monolithic platform, you get a focused workspace: when you log in, you are automatically directed to the areas your account has enabled — so your team never has to wade through features they don't use.

## Available Modules

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Duka — Point of Sale" icon="cash-register" href="/concepts/modules#duka-point-of-sale">

Duka handles your sales floor end-to-end: table management, cart and order creation, kitchen routing, product and category setup, inventory tracking, purchase orders, supplier management, delivery notes, gift cards, and itemized sales reports. Works across restaurant, retail, hospital, and hotel modes.

Pesa covers chart of accounts, manual and automated journal entries, invoices and bills, credit and debit notes, bank statement import and categorization, bank reconciliation, and financial reports including profit & loss, balance sheet, trial balance, cash flow, VAT summary, and AR/AP ageing.

  <Card title="Mteja — CRM" icon="users" href="/concepts/modules#mteja-crm">

## How Modules Work

Module activation is managed at the account level. When a module is enabled, it becomes available to every user in your account. When it is disabled, those screens and features are hidden across the platform — no data is lost, the module is simply inactive.

This means you can have an accounting team member whose account never shows POS screens, and a cashier whose account never exposes accounting menus — all from the same platform.

## Combining Modules

Modules are designed to work together. For example:

- **Duka + Pesa** — every completed sale in Duka automatically posts journal entries into Pesa, so your books stay in sync with your POS.
- **Duka + Bandu** — staff attendance records in Bandu can be tied to shift schedules managed alongside your POS operations.
- **Duka + Mteja** — customer records created at the POS are shared with the CRM lead pipeline, giving your sales team a full purchase history.

<Tip>
  If you're unsure which modules fit your business, start with **Duka** and add **Pesa** once your team is comfortable with daily sales operations. You can layer in CRM, HR, and Real Estate as your needs grow — there's no need to configure everything on day one.
</Tip>
